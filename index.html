Global Impact

Why This Matters to Europe & The World

The Iranian regime's actions have direct consequences for global security and stability

Drone Shipments to Russia

Iran acknowledged supplying drones to Russia before the Ukraine war. EU has widened sanctions against Iran for supporting Russia's aggression.

Reuters Al Jazeera EU Council Euronews

US Sanctions on Iran

US unveiled new sanctions against Iran-linked drone component suppliers. Additional sanctions target Houthi funding networks.

Sanctions Evasion & Oil

Despite international sanctions, Iran continues to export oil through shadow fleets, undermining global efforts to limit the regime's funding.

Some of the Victims

Real people with dreams, families, and futures stolen by the regime

Zahra (Raha) Bohlouli-Pour

18 years old • Student, Italian Literature

Killed on January 8, 2026 at Fatemi Sq, Tehran. Shot in the head with a Kalashnikov-style rifle. She studied at the University of Tehran.

Source

Arnika Dabbagh

15 years old • Swimming Champion

Killed on January 9, 2026 in Gorgan, northern Iran. A three-time swimming champion who dreamed of competing in the Olympics.

Source

Arya Alidoust

28 years old • Gilaki Citizen

Killed on January 8, 2026 in Lahijan, northern Iran. Shot by motorbike units of regime forces using live ammunition.

Source

Ali Abbasi

18 years old • Laborer from Kurdistan

Killed on January 7, 2026 in Tehran. Originally from Gilan-e Gharb, Kurdistan. His body was buried in his hometown.

Source

Mohammad Mardokhi

Political Activist • Mariwan, Kurdistan

Published an open letter critical of Khamenei on Jan 7. Found dead on Jan 13 in Mariwan with signs of torture, broken legs.
